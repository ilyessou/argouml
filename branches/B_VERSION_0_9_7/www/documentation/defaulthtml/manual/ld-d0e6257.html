A typical constraints tab. Along the top from the left are icons for creating a new constraint, deleting a constraint, editing an existing constraint, validating a constraint and altering the OCL parameters. To the right of these is a tick box for the Syntax Assistant. Beneath to the left is a panel titled Constraint Name (partially obscured) with beneath it a list of constraints (two in this example). Two small arrows the upper pointing left and the lower pointing right are to the right of this panel. The upper arrow shrinks the panel to nothing, the lower expands it to the whole tab. Finally to the right is a panel titled Preview containing the text of the constraint.
