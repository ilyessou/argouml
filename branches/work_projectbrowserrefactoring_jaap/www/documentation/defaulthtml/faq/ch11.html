11.1 To Do

How can I help?

You can help by joining the ArgoUML project.

On the ArgoUML web site there is a list of things to do in Issues.Issuezilla is active and it is highly recommended that you use it when you find something that 'doesn't quite work' in Argo the way you'd like it to.

The project needs persons skilled in several different fields such as:

  • Programming Java, C++...

  • Testing

  • Writing documentation

  • Proofreading documentation

  • UML

A good place to start is to take a look at the To Do list.The Job Jar

Download the CVS take a look at the code.http://argouml.tigris.org/documentation/projectsource/index.html

I don't have CVS access but I have an update or patch I'd like to submit. How do I do that?

Contact any of the active developers on the list and send them your updates for review and inclusion.
