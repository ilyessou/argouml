ArgoUML Presentation Tab

ArgoUML's Presentation Tab allows you to edit the visual properties of the selected design element. The contents of this tab pane may vary considerably depending on the selected design element. For example, in the figure above, which is the presentation tab for a class, you can toggle the visibility of the path in front of the class-name, toggle the visibility of the attributes and operations compartiments, set the line and filling color, and adjust the way stereotypes are represented: textual or by icon.
