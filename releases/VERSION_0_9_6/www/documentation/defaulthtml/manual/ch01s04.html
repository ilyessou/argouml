1.4. Assumptions

The current version of the manual assumes the reader is very familiar with UML already. This is reflected in the sparseness of the description of UML concepts in the tutorial.

The case study is described, but not yet fully realized throughout the tutorial. This will be achieved in future versions of the manual.
