5.12. Navigator pane

Purpose - to provide a tree view of the diagrams and objects in the model.

The Navigator pane will be located in org.argouml.?

The Navigator pane is a Layer 2 component. See Section 4.5.

Currently the Navigator pane is mixed with the GUI framework and actions in org.argouml.ui. Parts of it (Go rules) are mixed with Diagrams and Property panels under org.argouml.uml.diagram.ui and org.argouml.uml.diagram.diagram.ui.
