Max Talanov (2007)

Title: DSD for ArgoUML
Author: Max Talanov
Language: English
Abstract:

ArgoUML is small, open source, cross platform UML tool, though it is very young it is powerful modeling tool with impressive reverse engineering options.

ArgoUML can be extended for complex solution of modeling task, with UML, DSD and ORM options.
