Max Talanov (2007)

Title: ORM diagrams intro
Author: Max Talanov
Language: English
Abstract:

Author proposes UML based notation for ORM diagrams to make Object-relational mapping a part of application design. Article consists of description of main notation constructs and three variants of representation: views.

Author proposes ArgoUML as a good base for further research of the complex UML-ORM-DSD platform, which is important in modern IT industry.

"ArgoUML is small, open source, cross platform UML tool, though it is very young it is powerful modeling tool with impressive reverse engineering options."
