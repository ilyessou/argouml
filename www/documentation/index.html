The work with these documents continues. We are still looking for volunteers to help completing and improving these manuals. Not-yet-released versions are available for the purpose of proof-reading and discussion among the authors and contributers. You can look at them if you are interested to take part or monitor the progress. The snap shots are: Manual, and Quick guide. Voice your opinions about the direction and progress of these documents on the developers' mailing list after joining.
