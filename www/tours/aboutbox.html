Argo/UML About Box

Argo's About Box shows product information in the following tabs:

  • Splash: shows the splash screen that opens when the product launches.
  • Version: shows the product version number, build date, and component version information.
  • Credits: lists people who contributed to the development of Arg/UML.
  • Contact Info: email addresses to use for questions and comments on Argo/UML.
  • Legal: copyright information.
