ArgoUML Project Mailing Lists

To subscribe or unsubscribe, send an email message to the email address associated with each "subscribe" or "unsubscribe" line below. The subject line and message body are not used, so you may leave them blank.

Digests are a single message with of all discussion on a given day, arranged by thread.

announce@argouml: Announcements of new releases and other major project events.
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: announce-subscribe@argouml.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: announce-unsubscribe@argouml.tigris.org
 
users@argouml: The list for users, to ask questions or make wishes. Please look at the users FAQ before posting, thanks!
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: users-subscribe@argouml.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: users-unsubscribe@argouml.tigris.org
 
dev@argouml: The main list for developers to discuss development issues, and general discussion of UML and Visual Modeling as relevant to ArgoUML. Please do not ask questions about how to start ArgoUML or how to use this or that feature here, do that on users@argouml.tigris.org after concerning the users FAQ
