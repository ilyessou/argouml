ArgoUML Project Mailing Lists

Previously, the ArgoUML project hosted its mailing lists at another service provider, eGroups.com. Interested developers now have a choice as to where to subscribe and where to post.

To subscribe or unsubscribe, send an email message to the email address associated with each "subscribe" or "unsubscribe" line below. The subject line and message body are not used, so you may leave them blank.

announce@argouml: Announcements of new releases and other major project events.
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: announce-subscribe@argouml.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: announce-unsubscribe@argouml.tigris.org
 
dev@argouml: The main list for developers to discuss development issues, and general discussion of UML and Visual Modeling as relevant to ArgoUML.
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: dev-subscribe@argouml.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: dev-unsubscribe@argouml.tigris.org
 
bugs@argouml: Automated notifications of bugs reported against argouml itself.
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: bugs-subscribe@argouml.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: bugs-unsubscribe@argouml.tigris.org
 
cvs@argouml: Automated notifications of source code changes.
Archive: view on-line
Subscribe: cvs-subscribe@argouml.tigris.org
Unsubscribe: cvs-unsubscribe@argouml.tigris.org
