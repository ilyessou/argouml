ArgoUML Logo and T-Shirt Design Contest

A really cool software project deserves a really cool T-shirt!

Help support the ArgoUML project by showing your style and imagination. Design a ArgoUML T-shirt with an original logo. Your design will be displayed on this page for all to see. In the coming weeks we will have a vote on which one to use. The plan is for everyone who contributes to get a free T-shirt.

Here's what to do:

  • Save the blank shirt images by using a pop-up menu in your browser.
  • Use any graphics editor to add a logo to the shirt front or back or both. Don't be shy about your graphical abilities: if you have a good idea, I will help you refine it.
  • Consider using logos that you find on the web that relate to Greek Myth, sailing, object-oriented design, human cognition, automation, visualization, open-source, etc.
  • Good places for logos or text include: over the heart, on the sleve, across the front, across the back, the top-center of the back.
  • You may change the shirt color or size if you like.
  • Send email to jrobbins at collab.net with your images as enclosures.
