The User Manual and Quick Guide are the ones provided with the 0.9.6 release of ArgoUML. This is also the first release of the Quick Guide and User Manual and the User Manual is not complete. It is however already very helpful in a lot of areas.

The work with these documents continues. We are still looking for volunteers to help completing and improving these manuals. Not-yet-released versions are available for the purpose of proof-reading and discussion among the authors and contributers. You can look at them if you are interested to take part or monitor the progress. The snap shots are: Manual, Quick guide, and FAQ. Voice your opinions about the direction and progress of these documents on the developers' mailing list.
