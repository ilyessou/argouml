1.2. Scope of This User Manual

1.2.1. Target Audience

The current version of this document is aimed at experienced users of UML in OOA&D (perhaps with other tools) who wish to transfer to ArgoUML.

Future versions will support designers who know OOA&D, and wish to adopt a UML based process.

A long term goal is to support those who are learning design and wish to start with a UML based OOA&D process and people interested in modularized code design with a GUI.

1.2.2. Scope

The intention is that this document will provide a comprehensive guide, enabling designers to use ArgoUML to its full extent. It is in two parts.

  • A tutorial manual, showing how to work with ArgoUML

  • A complete reference manual, recording everything you can do with ArgoUML.

The current version of the document has achieved the second of these.

In this guide there are some things you will not find

  • Descriptions of how ArgoUML works on the inside.

  • How to improve ArgoUML with new features and functions.

  • A trouble shooting guide.

  • A summary quick reference to using ArgoUML.

These are covered elsewhere, particularly in the FAQ, Quick Guide and Developers Cookbook.
