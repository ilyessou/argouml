ArgoUML About Box

ArgoUML's About Box shows product information in the following tabs:

  • Splash: shows the splash screen that opens when the product launches.
  • Version: shows the product version number, build date, and component version information.
  • Credits: lists people who contributed to the development of ArgUML.
  • Contact Info: email addresses to use for questions and comments on ArgoUML.
  • Report bugs: how to report bugs.
  • Legal: copyright information.
