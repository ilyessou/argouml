ArgoUML Details Pane

ArgoUML's Details Pane allows you to edit the details of the currently selected design element or "to do" item. The ToDoItem tab has an westward arrowhead to indicate that it shows details of objects selected in the "To Do" Pane. Most of the other tabs have north facing arrowheads to indicate that they show details of objects selected in the diagram or explorer.

You can make the Details Pane larger by dragging on the dividers.
