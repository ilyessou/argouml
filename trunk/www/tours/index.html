Interactive demonstration

Here are some interactive demonstrations illustrating key concepts of ArgoUML in a hopefully helpful way.

The technique used is called Viewlets and your ordinary JavaApplet-enabled browser will be enough to view it. If you've not seen Viewlets before then you are in for a pleasant surprise.

  • Roundtrip of ArgoUML A somewhat random walk through Argo. See below.
