Add more critics, clarifiers, and wizards

Description: 

Implement critics to deliver knowledge from the following sources: all constraints in the UML semantic specs, design patterns books and articles, common coding conventions, books and articles on OO design heuristics. The current critics mainly cover the class diagrams, more are needed to support designers with other aspects of design. New clarifiers may be needed to highlight errors on new view elements. Wizards may be implemented to automate or guide correction of certain design errors.
