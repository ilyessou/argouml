7.2. Overview of the Window

Figure 7.1. Overview of the ArgoUML window shows the main ArgoUML window.

Figure 7.1. Overview of the ArgoUML window

At the top of screen is a menu bar described in Chapter 12. The Menu bar. The bulk of the window comprises four sub-windows or panes. Clockwise from top left these are the navigator pane (see Chapter 8. The Navigation Pane), editing pane (see Chapter 9. The Editing Pane), details pane (see Chapter 10. The Details Pane) and to-do pane (see Chapter 11. The To-Do Pane). At the top of the editing pane is a tool bar. An overview of the panes is given in Section 7.4. Finally at the bottom of the window is a status bar described in Section 7.5.
