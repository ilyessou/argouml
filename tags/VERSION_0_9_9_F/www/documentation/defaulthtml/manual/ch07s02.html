7.2. Overview of the Window

Figure 7.1 shows the main ArgoUML window.

Figure 7.1. Overview of the ArgoUML window

At the top of screen is a menu bar described in Chapter 12. The bulk of the window comprises four sub-windows or panes. Clockwise from top left these are the navigator pane (see Chapter 8), editing pane (see Chapter 9), details pane (see Chapter 10) and to-do pane (see Chapter 11). At the top of the editing pane is a tool bar. An overview of the panes is given in Section 7.5. Finally at the bottom of the window is a status bar described in Section 7.6.
